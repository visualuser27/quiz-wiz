    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I create my own quiz on this platform?

    Yes! You can easily create your own quiz using our simple quiz creation tool. Just click on the "Generate your own quiz!" button in the navigation bar to get started.

    Is registration required to take quizzes?

    No, you can take quizzes without registering. However, creating an account lets you save your progress and track your results.

    Are there rewards for completing quizzes?

    Yes, we have a leaderboard system where top performers are recognized, and you can earn badges for completing specific milestones!

    Can I access quizzes on mobile devices?

    Absolutely! Our platform is mobile-friendly and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets.

    How is my data protected?

    We prioritize your data security with encryption and privacy-focused settings. Check our privacy policy for more details.
